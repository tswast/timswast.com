0hgame 2016 - Go Fish!

Tim Swast on Thursday, November 10, 2016

This post contains interactive elements and is best experienced with a web browser at the original post. I had a late entry to the 0-hour game jam this year. I didn't feel like getting up at 1AM, but I still wanted to use my "extra" hour to make a game this year. I built a game of Go Fish!. Enjoy!

It doesn't use any images, only the Unicode characters for playing cards and a few emoji. It ended up taking me maybe 4 or 5 hours instead of 0 or 1 (which surprised me a bit, since it's such a simple game). Hopefully the next card game I make will be a bit easier with this as an example.
